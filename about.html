Our Mission

At EcoCutlery, we believe that small changes can make a big difference. Our mission is to eliminate single-use plastic cutlery by providing delicious, sustainable alternatives that don't compromise on functionality or taste.

Every year, billions of plastic utensils end up in landfills and oceans. We're changing that narrative by creating edible cutlery that's not just environmentally friendly, but also adds flavor to your dining experience.

Our Journey

2020

The Idea

Sarah Chen conceptualized edible cutlery after a beach cleanup revealed the massive scale of plastic pollution.

2021

First Prototype

After months of research, we created our first edible spoon using wheat flour and natural binding agents.

2022

Product Launch

EcoCutlery officially launched with three flavors of edible spoons, receiving overwhelming positive response.

2023

Expansion

Expanded product line to include forks, knives, and combo packs. Partnered with 100+ restaurants.

2024

Global Impact

Reached 50+ countries and prevented over 2.5 million plastic utensils from entering the environment.
